Introduction

In the world of reinforcement learning, there has not been much work done while considering how teams can work together to maximize both individual and collective reward.
This website aims to provide a visual aid in analyzing data from such a simulation. In this simulation Two teams are considered, referred to as Team 1 and Team 2 respectively.
Each team player is allowed to freely move about the playing field where they select actions From a limited action space. The actions are defined by a policy function which in
future iterations will be constructed to maximize reward. Players from opposing teams can also interact by "stealing" resources such as health and fuel. The objective of the game
is for each team to collect all items assigned to it. The first team to do so is declared the winner.
